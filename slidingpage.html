Things you’ll need:

• Playstation 3 Console on Firmware 3.55 or Lower

• USB Flashdrive, 8GB Minimum - 16GB Preferred

• Computer or Laptop with a Stable Internet Connection

What to do:

STEP 1: Download CFW (Custom Firmware) 3.55 from the "Downloads" Section.

STEP 2: Format your USB Flashdirve to "Fat32". You can use a tool called "Rufus" provided in the Downloads Section (Use Portable download link if you don't want to install it).

STEP 3: Once Formatted, Make a folder titled "PS3" and another titled "UPDATE" inside "PS3". Rename your downloaded CFW file to "PS3UPDAT.PUP" and copy this file onto your USB in the "UPDATE" folder. The directory should be PS3>UPDATE>PS3UPDAT.PUP.

WARNING: TURN THE INTERNET CONNECTION OFF ON THE PS3 NOW AND LEAVE IT ON MANUAL. (Check FAQ for details)

STEP 4: At this stage we've left no room for irreversible accidents by turning the internet off. Once Offline, Plug your USB into the PS3, on your XMB scroll to "System Update" > "Update" > "via Storage Media" > "Install".

STEP 5: Wait for the installation process, do NOT turn off the console at any point during the process. (Check FAQ for details)

STEP 6: Done, Now check if you are on the correct version by going into "Settings", scroll down to "System Information" and look at your current firmware.



Hacked! Lets install some protection packages:

Step 1: Download "SenEnabler", "PsnPatch" & "Multiman" from the "Downloads" Section.

Step 2: Paste these 2 files in the root directory of your USB, no need to make folders.

Step 3: Plug the USB back into your console, go to the "Games" drop down in your XMB and look for "Install Pkg" or "Install Packages", in there you can choose to install "Pkg" from USB or Storage Media, Install all 3 packages we put in the USB.

Step 4: Now you'll find these 3 packages as Executable apps in your "Game" section.

Step 5: Congrats! You did it! You've modified your console, installed 3 very important packages and now you're ready to download free games, make copies of your current disc versions onto the HDD and hack games!
